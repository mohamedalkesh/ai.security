About The Project

An intelligent platform built to defend modern networks

What is MADRS?

MADRS (MITRE ATT&CK Detection and Response System) is an AI-powered incident response platform designed to help organizations detect, analyze, and contain cyber threats in real time. It combines machine learning models, threat intelligence feeds, and the MITRE ATT&CK framework to give security teams full visibility over attacks targeting their infrastructure.

What does it do?

  • Detects intrusions and suspicious behavior automatically using AI models
  • Classifies threats and maps them to MITRE ATT&CK tactics and techniques
  • Triggers automated response actions to contain incidents instantly
  • Generates detailed incident reports and analytics dashboards
  • Monitors network traffic and endpoints 24/7 with low-latency alerts

Our Mission

Make advanced cybersecurity accessible by automating threat detection and response with AI.
